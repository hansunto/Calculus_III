Corollary 5.40. Area Formulas via Green’s Theorem.

Green’s Theorem yields the following equivalent line integral formulas for calculating the geometric area \(A\) of a planar region \(D\) along its positively oriented boundary curve \(C\text{:}\)
\begin{equation*} A = \oint_C x\,dy = -\oint_C y\,dx = \frac{1}{2}\oint_C x\,dy - y\,dx. \end{equation*}
